Clipboard History

— Clipboard List —

Clipboard History allows quick access to your most recently copied or cut text.

To add text to your clipboard, just use Copy or Cut.

To paste text from the clipboard, select the menu item Edit→Paste From Clipboard History or its keyboard shortcut:

Click on the text you want to use and it will be pasted into your last selected window and the dialog will close automatically.

You can also just type the character shown for the entry to select it.
